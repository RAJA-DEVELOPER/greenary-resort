Review of the Month

A Story Worth Reading

SC
Sarah Chen
Wildlife Biologist · California, USA
Grand Ridge Traverse + Canopy Treehouse
October 2024 · 5 Nights

"I study ecosystems professionally. I have worked in the Serengeti, the Amazon, and the Himalayas. I say this not to boast but to provide context for what follows: Terravana Wilds is the single most profound wildlife and wilderness experience I have ever had. The guides don't just know the animals - they know their individual stories. On day two of the Ridge Traverse, our guide stopped and pointed to a set of tracks. 'That's the female we call Asha,' he said quietly. 'She's been using this trail since 2018.' Forty minutes later, Asha the leopard walked past us at fifteen metres, looked directly into our eyes, and continued into the undergrowth. I sat down on a rock and couldn't speak for ten minutes. That is Terravana."

About Our Reviews

Are these reviews verified

Yes. Every review labeled "Verified Stay" is cross-checked against our booking database. We do not publish unverified reviews on our direct site.

Do you delete negative reviews

No. We display our direct feed from TripAdvisor and Google, which we cannot edit. We respond to all feedback publicly and use criticism to improve the lodge.

Can I contact a past guest

For privacy reasons, we cannot share guest contact info. However, many of our guests are active in our Terravana Community Facebook group, where you can ask questions directly.
