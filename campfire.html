Wildfire Glow

The forest turns amber after dark.

Our evening fire circle is cinematic, warm, and carefully controlled: ember light, smoked food, local stories, acoustic quiet, and a night sky that feels close enough to touch.

Fire Circle

Low seating, safe fire bowls, warm blankets, and a host who sets the rhythm of the night.

Flame Kitchen

Smoked vegetables, charred breads, spiced broths, forest herb tea, and seasonal sweets.

Night Walk

Optional pre-fire walk for owls, frogs, cicadas, and the strange music of the dark canopy.

Stargazing

Clear evenings become sky sessions with constellations, folklore, and warm cups in hand.

Fire Camp Setup

Built for warmth, stories, and safe wonder.

Every campfire setup is arranged before sunset, checked by the host, and tuned for comfort: low chairs, warm throws, lantern paths, and enough space for the forest to keep breathing.

Circle Seating

Lantern Path

Hosted Safety

Trained hosts monitor wind, fuel, guest distance, ash, and final extinguishing.

Cinematic Fire Circle

All glow, no hurry.

The scene is dramatic, but the footprint is careful. Every fire is hosted in a designated zone, monitored by trained staff, and closed with a strict ash, water, and cleanup protocol.

Soundscape

The night has its own soundtrack.

Campfire nights are not loud entertainment. They are designed around forest sound: crackle, insects, night birds, breath, soft conversation, and acoustic music that never fights the place.

Acoustic Sets

Low-volume folk songs, hand drums, and simple strings around the circle.

Forest Quiet

Silent pauses for night calls, wind shifts, and the tiny clicks of cooling wood.

Local Lore

Stories of rivers, old trails, monsoon lights, and the animals that share the valley.

Night Watch

Guides help identify distant calls without disturbing wildlife movement.

Is it suitable for children

Yes. We keep the early part family friendly and can arrange quieter seating for younger guests.

What happens if it rains

The experience moves to a covered fire kitchen or lodge hearth depending on weather.

Can food be customised

Yes. Tell us your dietary needs during booking and the kitchen will plan accordingly.

Is the fire low impact

We use designated fire areas, controlled fuel, and strict cleanup protocols after every evening.
