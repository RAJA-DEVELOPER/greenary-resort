Common Questions

Booking FAQs

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for standard stays, and 8-12 weeks for peak season (October-February). The Canopy Treehouses and Ridge Villas sell out earliest.
The Full Terravana Package includes lodge accommodation, all breakfasts, 3 guided adventure days (choice of trail/safari/waterfall), daily forest herb tea, one campfire night, and a welcome gift. Flights and travel insurance are not included.
Children aged 7+ are welcome on easy and moderate trails, campfire nights, and the birding experience. High-altitude and challenging treks are recommended for ages 14+. Wildlife safaris welcome all ages. Our team will advise on the best programme for your family.
Our reservations team will review your request and send a detailed quote to your email within 24 hours (usually much sooner). No payment is required until you confirm the booking via our secure payment link. We'll also call to discuss your itinerary preferences.
We strongly recommend travel insurance with adventure activity coverage for all guests. This should cover emergency medical evacuation, trip cancellation, and any outdoor activities. We can recommend providers if needed.

What Your Days Look Like

A Sample 5-Day Terravana Journey

01
Day
Arrival & Forest Immersion

Private transfer from Coimbatore. Check in to your lodge. Guided forest walk at dusk with your naturalist. Welcome campfire dinner under the stars.

02
Day
Dawn Wildlife Safari

4:30am wake-up. Pre-dawn safari into the wildlife corridor. Leopard, sloth bear, and Nilgiri tahr sighting possibilities. Return for forest-to-table breakfast.

03
Day
Grand Ridge Traverse

Full-day ridge trek to 3,100m. Above the clouds by midday. Summit lunch. Return via eastern waterfall valley with wild swimming at Devakund Pool.

04
Day
Free Exploration & Astronomer Night

Morning at your own pace - birdwatching, forest journaling, or spa treatment. Evening: Astronomer's Campfire with our resident dark-sky specialist.

05
Day
Departure & Conservation Walk

Final morning guided walk through the rewilding zone - see the trees your booking planted. Departure by noon with forest-sourced gifts and your personal trail map.

Prepare Well

What to Bring

Pack light - the forest provides the rest. Below is our recommended packing list for a 3-5 day visit.

Footwear

Sturdy ankle-support hiking boots. One pair of camp sandals. Waterproof if visiting June-September.

Clothing

Moisture-wicking layers. Waterproof shell jacket. Fleece for evenings and early mornings at altitude.

Photography

Camera with telephoto lens (200mm+ for wildlife). Portable charger. All batteries charged before arrival - no mains in the forest.

Leave Behind

Plastic bags, single-use bottles, and synthetic fragrances. We provide refillable steel bottles and biodegradable soap for all guests.
